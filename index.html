Websites have too much going on now a days. There's social media, but you're restricted by their UI and algorithms.

So I am making mine like it's 1999. No need for any Node.js, Next, or React... or this or that package. Just pure HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

I want to share my interest and write essays on here. I have journals filled with writing. This is a directory for me, my work, my thoughts, goals for the future, and memories of the past.

No chasing any views or likes. A digitial journal. A homepage that is home.

This site is a lot too but in a different way. I am not the first person to feel this way and I have some site inspirations.
